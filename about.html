The Maison Royale Story

Founded in the heart of Paris, Royale Cart began as a small atelier dedicated to creating exceptional leather goods and accessories. Inspired by the golden age of French luxury, our founder, Isabelle Moreau, envisioned a house that celebrates timeless sophistication without pretense.

Today, we collaborate with master artisans across Italy, France, and Spain — each piece bearing the mark of meticulous attention, from hand-stitched seams to ethically sourced materials. Every Royale Cart item tells a story of passion, integrity, and understated splendor.

Our mission: To craft treasures that empower self-expression, while championing sustainable luxury. We are a carbon-neutral brand and donate 2% of profits to artisans’ education.

From Atelier to You

Every Royale Cart piece begins its journey as a raw sketch, then evolves through dozens of hands — pattern makers, leather cutters, seamstresses, and finishers. Our atelier in the Marais district echoes with the quiet rhythm of dedication. We use only full-grain leathers, natural linings, and precious metals free from conflict.

We invite you to explore our “Made to Order” program, where you can co-create personalized pieces with our artisans. Because true luxury is not mass-produced — it’s imagined, nurtured, and delivered with a royal touch.
