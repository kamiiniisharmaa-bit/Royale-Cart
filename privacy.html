3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used only for legitimate business interests and legal obligations. In particular:

  • To fulfill orders: process payments, arrange shipping, provide invoices and order confirmations.
  • To manage your account: maintain loyalty rewards, save preferences, and provide wishlist functionality.
  • To communicate with you: respond to inquiries, send service emails (order updates, delivery alerts), and notify about changes to terms or policies.
  • For marketing (with consent): send you newsletters, personalized offers, and early access to collections. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in our emails.
  • To improve our services: analyze usage trends, diagnose technical issues, and enhance user experience.
  • Fraud prevention & security: detect and prevent fraudulent transactions, cyber attacks, and unauthorized access.

5. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data. However, we share information with trusted third parties only for operational purposes:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), shipping carriers (DHL, FedEx), IT hosting, and email delivery platforms. They only receive data necessary to perform their functions and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, we may disclose information to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred, subject to continued protection under this policy.
Royal Confidentiality Pledge: We never share your personal information with third parties for their own direct marketing without your explicit consent.

6. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and restricted access to personal data. Payment card information is tokenized and not stored on our servers after authorization. Despite our efforts, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary: for active accounts (duration of your relationship with Royale Cart), to comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records for 7 years), and for legitimate business purposes (fraud prevention, analytics). After that, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA, & Global)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention periods.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of direct marketing or processing based on legitimate interests.
  • California Privacy Rights (CCPA): California residents may request disclosure of categories of personal information collected, deletion, and opt-out of “sale” of data (we do not sell).

To exercise any rights, please contact privacy@royalecart.com or call our Privacy Desk at +33 1 88 88 40 21. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., CNIL in France).

8. Children’s Privacy

Royale Cart does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 16. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may contact us to request removal.

9. International Data Transfers

As a global luxury brand, we operate internationally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including France (our headquarters) and the United States (for cloud hosting). When we transfer data from the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequacy decisions. By using our services, you understand that your information may be transferred to our facilities and those third parties with whom we share it as described in this policy.
